Great Wolf Lodge


Indoor Water Park: Great Wolf Lodge features an indoor water park that operates year-round, regardless of the weather. Size: The water park typically spans over 80,000 square feet. Attractions: Includes a variety of slides, a wave pool, lazy river, and interactive water play areas. Kid-Friendly: Designed with families in mind, it offers attractions for all age groups, including smaller slides for toddlers. Accommodation Packages: Entry to the water park is included with an overnight stay:

Disney World


Typhoon Lagoon: Features a large wave pool, a lazy river, and a variety of slides. Known for its tropical theme. Surf Pool: One of the largest wave pools in the world. Kid's Areas: Includes a dedicated area for younger children. Blizzard Beach: Designed as a winter wonderland, it boasts some of the tallest and fastest slides. Summit Plummet: One of the tallest free-fall slides in the U.S. Family Raft Rides: Offers several attractions that can be enjoyed with the whole family. Seasonal Operations: Both parks are typically open seasonally, with hours varying by season

Universal Orlando


(Volcano Bay) Themed Water Park: The park is themed around a lush, tropical setting with a central volcano. Utilizes a virtual queue system that allows guests to reserve access to rides and attractions. Variety of Attractions: Includes high-speed slides, a lazy river, and a massive wave pool. Family-Friendly: Offers attractions suitable for all ages, including a dedicated kids' area. Dining Options: Features a variety of dining venues, from quick-service to sit-down restaurants.

Six Flags America


Six Flags has water parks in various locations, each with unique themes and attractions. Thrill Rides: Known for their high-thrill water rides, including speed slides and multi-lane racing attractions. Family Areas: Most parks include areas specifically designed for younger children and families. Seasonal Operations: Typically open from late spring to early fall, with some parks offering extended hours on weekends. Admission: Many Six Flags water parks offer combo tickets with their amusement parks for a full day of fun.
